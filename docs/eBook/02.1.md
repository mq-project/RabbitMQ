windows上面的安装
===================

下载地址： http://www.rabbitmq.com/releases/rabbitmq-server/v3.6.9/rabbitmq-server-3.6.9.exe

* 双击exe文件，安装opt，
* 安装RabbitMQ

C:\Program Files\RabbitMQ Server\rabbitmq_server-3.6.9\sbin  放入到环境变量中


rabbitmq-plugins enable rabbitmq_management


http://127.0.0.1:15672/  guest:guest
